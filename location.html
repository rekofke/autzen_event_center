Location

Autzen Stadium is located in Eugene, Oregon on the University of Oregon campus. The stadium is home to the Oregon Ducks football team and is also a popular venue for concerts and other events.

2700 Martin Luther King Jr Blvd, Eugene, OR 97401

About Autzen Stadium

Autzen Stadium is the home of the Oregon Ducks football team. The stadium was built in 1967 and has a seating capacity of 54,000. The stadium is known for its loud and raucous atmosphere, which has earned it the nickname "The House of Loud." Autzen Stadium is also known for its unique design, which features a covered grandstand that helps to amplify crowd noise.
